Welcome to Odin Recipes

Welcome to the Odin Recipes website. A site designed by me as a tool to help deepen my understanding of HTML and CSS. I orignally had some trouble understanding CSS. All the different keywords made very little sense to me and I had trouble even just trying to two elements to appear next to each other. But, through this projects I feel as though I have grown as a developer and that I now understand and know more about HTML and CSS. I hope to use this as a starting point in my efforts to learn web-development.
