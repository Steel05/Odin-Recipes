Cinnamon Roll Dump Cake

Prep Time:

15 mins

Bake Time:

40 mins

Cool Time:

10 mins

Servings:

12 portions

Ingredients

  • 17.5 ounces premade cinnamon roll dough
  • 16 ounces cream cheese frosting
  • 0.5 cup toasted pecans
  • 21.5 ounces cinnamon swirl coffee cake mix
  • 0.75 cup salted butter

Nutrition Facts

  • 799 calories
  • 40g fat
  • 105g carbs
  • 6g protien

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) and prep 9 by 13 inch baking pan.
  2. Cut cinnamon rolls into 1.5 inch pieces and place evenly in baking pan.
  3. Remove foil lining from frosting and microwave for 5 to 15 seconds.
  4. Spread frosting and pecans evenly over cut cinnamon rolls.
  5. Sprinkle cake mix evenly over cut cinnamon rolls and add butter slices across mixture.
  6. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes or until puffed and golden.
