Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil noodles: Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil, then add the noodles. Remove when al dente. Once done, drain and transfer to a prepared baking pan.
  2. Make the cheese sauce: Melt butter, then whisk in flour. Mix until combined. Once mixed, add milk and mix until combined. After, bring the mixture to a simmer, and stir in cheeses. Add salt and pepper and let simmer until thick. Finally, pour the sauce over the noodles.
  3. Make the topping: Melt two tablespoons of butter, add breadcrumbs, and toast until brown. Sprinkle over the noodles and sauce, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese: Bake in a preheated oven until the topping turns golden brown.
