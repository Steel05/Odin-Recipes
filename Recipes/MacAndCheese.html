Mac and Cheese

Prep Time:

10 mins

Cook Time:

45 mins

Servings:

4 portions

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Seasoning

Nutrition Facts

  • 845 calories
  • 48g fat
  • 65g carbs
  • 37g protein

Directions

  1. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil, then add the noodles. Remove when al dente. Once done, drain and transfer to a prepared baking pan.
  2. Melt butter, then whisk in flour. Mix until combined. Once mixed, add milk and mix until combined. After, bring the mixture to a simmer, and stir in cheeses. Add salt and pepper and let simmer until thick. Finally, pour the sauce over the noodles.
  3. Melt two tablespoons of butter, add breadcrumbs, and toast until brown. Sprinkle over the noodles and sauce, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until the topping turns golden brown.
