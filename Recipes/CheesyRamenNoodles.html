Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Cook Time:

5 mins

Servings:

1 portion

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Ramen package
  • American cheese

Nutrition Facts

  • 163 calories
  • 11g fat
  • 8g carbs
  • 8g protein

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, then add noodles. Let cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  2. Pour out water and add seasoning packet to noodles. Mix until well blended.
  3. Add cheese and stir until well combined.
