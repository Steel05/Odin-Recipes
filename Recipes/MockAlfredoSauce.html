Mock Alfredo Sauce

Cook Time:

20 mins

Servings:

8 portions

Ingredients:

  • 1 stick butter
  • 8 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 quart whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lbs pasta of choice
  • Salt and pepper

Nutrition Facts:

  • 793 calories
  • 12g fat
  • 48g carbs
  • 18g protein

Directions

  1. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat
  2. Begin boiling water for pasta, if it reaches a boil reduce to simmer
  3. Remove butter from heat and stir in flour to form roux
  4. Return roux to heat, do not allow browning or scorching
  5. After cooking for 5 minutes, pour in milk one cup at a time, whisking well between additions
  6. Add salt, pepper, nutmeg, and granulated garlic
  7. Simmer over medium heat for 10 minutes or until sauce thickens, whisk constantly
  8. Bring pasta water to rolling boil, add salt, and cook pasta until al dente
  9. Once cooked, set aside some water and drain the rest
  10. Remove sauce from heat and whisk in grated Parmesan cheese
  11. When served, if pasta is too dry, add pasta water to thin the sauce
