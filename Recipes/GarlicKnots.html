Garlic Knots

Prep Time:

20 mins

Cook Time:

10 mins

Cool Time:

5 mins

Servings:

8 portions

Ingredients

  • Self-rising flour
  • Whole fat plain Greek yogurt
  • Granulated garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Dried parsely
  • Garlic garnules

Nutrition Facts

  • 275 calories
  • 25g fat
  • 12g carbs
  • 2g protien

Directions

  1. Combine flour, yogurt, Italian seasoning, and salt in a bowl. Mix to combine.
  2. Dust work surface with flour. Transfer dough to work surface.Knead the dough for 8 to 10 minutes. Add more flour as needed to keep dough from being too sticky.
  3. Shape dough into rounded loaf. Then, cut off 8 wedges of dough. Roll each wedge into a rope shape and tie into knots. Place knots onto prepared baking sheet.
  4. Place knots in a 500 degrees F (260 degrees C) oven. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned.
  5. In a small bowl, combine melted butter, oil, garlic garnules, and parsely.
  6. When done, remove knots from oven and brush with topping.
