Pink Sauce

Cook Time:

15 mins

Servings:

4 portions

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup dairy product
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 1/2 lbs pasta of choice
  • Salt and pepper

Nutrition Facts:

  • 793 calories
  • 12g fat
  • 48g carbs
  • 18g protein

Directions

  1. Begin boiling water for pasta, if it reaches a boil reduce to simmer
  2. Heat oil over medium heat
  3. Add tomatoes to oil and stir well
  4. Add salt and pepper and stir
  5. Cook over low heat for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally
  6. Add salt to pasta water then add pasta
  7. Cook pasta until al dente
  8. Once cooked, set aside some water and drain the rest
  9. Remove sauce from heat and add dairy product and stir until smooth
  10. Add ricotta cheese and stir well
  11. Return sauce to heat and bring to a boil
  12. Shut off heat and add in parmesan cheese
  13. When served, if pasta is too dry, add pasta water to thin the sauce
